About Pleach Shop

Bridging sellers and millions of customers through a premium e-commerce platform.

Our Vision

To be India's most preferred e-commerce marketplace by creating hyper-efficient supply links and delivering delight to every pin code in the country.

Our Mission

To empower local micro-businesses, brands, and sellers to reach a national customer base, while providing buyers with authentic, quality-checked goods at unmatched prices.

Established in 2026, Pleach Shop started as a boutique retail aggregator and has rapidly grown into a multi-category marketplace serving millions of happy customers.
